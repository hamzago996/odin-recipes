Mansaf

What is Mansaf?

Mansaf is a tasty Palestinian dish which consists of yellow colored rice with chopped lamb, and the tastiest of them all--yogurt!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Yellow Onion
  • Special Yogurt

Preparation

  1. Make rice
  2. Add a cup of salmon
  3. Pour the special yogurt
  4. Enjoy!
